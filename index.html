2. Novice Debate

Sept. 22nd, 2018
Orosi HS

This tournament is for students who have never competed in a debate tournament before. Each student will debate four different teams.

3. I.E. #1

Oct. 6th, 2018
Clovis HS

This is the first of the three I.E. speech tournaments. I.E tournaments serve as a way for students to compete and test out their new speeches at a real tournament.

Oct. 13th, 2018
Edison HS

This is the 1st of 3 Wahzoo tournaments. This tournament has four debate rounds. Students' record from this tournament will carry over to Wahzoo 2.

Oct. 25th, 2018 - Oct. 28th, 2018
University of the Pacific (UOP)

This tournament only for select students. The tournament is three days long. Students must double enter in speech and debate, instead of competing in just one.

Nov. 3rd, 2018
Clovis North HS

CN Forensics is having a showcase open to the public; students will be performing or debating for the audience. Dinner will be served as well.

Nov. 10th, 2018
Sanger HS

This is the second of the three Wahzoo debate tournaments. Students' records from Wahzoo 1 determine the difficulty of competition at this tournament.

Clovis East HS

Dec. 8th, 2018
Clovis North HS
