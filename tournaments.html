4. Wahzoo #1 (Debate)

Oct. 13th, 2018
Edison HS

This is the first of the three Wahzoo debate tournaments (the name "Wahzoo" has no significance). Students' debate records from Wahzoo 1, 2, and 3 are all connected, as if it was one large Wahzoo tournament spread out into three separate tournaments. Each Wahzoo tournament has four rounds, meaning that a student's debate record will be out of 12 rounds by the end of Wahzoo 3. Students' records in the Wahzoo tournaments will determine their ranking at State Quals Debate. The I.E speech tournaments are not like the Wahzoo system; think of I.E tournaments as just practice for State Quals A/B Speech.
